The Event

The course is going to be about 8 hours with one lunch break and two coffee breaks. The class will be given by the girl who organizes the event in her city.

The content was created, but whoever wants to contribute please contact the JS4GIRLS facebook page.

The event will be monthly or bimonthly depending on the demand.
